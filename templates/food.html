FOOD

From a fresh fruit salad, to a bar of dark chocolate, many foods have been proven to help reduce stress and anxiety- and eating the right foods can help balance and restore your mind and body’s equilibrium. Whether it’s because of the reliable source of energy they provide, or the specific nutrients they give you, these foods can help you through the day feeling even and focused. Here are some foods that have been proven to do just that, including some of our personal favourites!
